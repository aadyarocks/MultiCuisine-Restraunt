We are a bistro serving global cuisine interpreted through an Indian lens. Over the last 10 Years, we have been serving an ever-evolving menu of casual comfort food that is created by a team of people who are passionate about discovering new tastes and ingredients, while staying true to our Indian roots.

We brings you exciting dishes, perfectly blended with a relaxed dining experience, making us the perfect place for a feast with family or just a quick bite on the go.
